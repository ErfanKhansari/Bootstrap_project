Corey Todd Taylor, popularly known as The Boogie Knight and The Grim Chopper, is an American singer, songwriter, musician, actor and author. He is best known as the lead vocalist and lyricist of the heavy metal band Slipknot as well as the hard rock band Stone Sour. He has also worked with other bands, including Korn, Disturbed, Junk Beer Kidnap Band, Anthrax, Apocalyptica and Soulfly. The talented musician has released several albums and has even acted as a guest musician on many. His famous collaborative works include the albums Soularium, Primitive, Rise Above, New Found Power, Give the Drummer Some and The Serenity of Suffering, to name a few. Taylor has also done a handful of movies, the popular ones being Fear Clinic and Bullied. Coming to the singers achievements, he had been ranked at position 86 in Hit Parader's list of Top 100 Metal Vocalists of All Time. He was also honored by NME as the 7th greatest heavy metal frontman. Not just this! He even holds the record of possessing the second-widest vocal range in contemporary popular music.
